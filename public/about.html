About Us

Building trust in the catering industry requires dedication, quality service, and a deep passion for what we do. For De Las Armas, this journey began in Baliwag, Bulacan, where we first established ourselves as a humble family business specializing in catering. What started as a small operation catering to intimate gatherings quickly grew into providing catering services for larger events, complete with tables, chairs, and elegant backdrops. Over the years, our reputation for quality and service has allowed us to expand, catering to both private functions and corporate events across Bulacan and nearby areas. Led by the passionate and hands-on team, De Las Armas is committed to ensuring that every event is a memorable experience for our clients. From the smallest details to the grandest celebrations, we guarantee top- notch service and food quality that our customers can rely on. The family's direct involvement in day-to-day operations means that client concerns are always addressed promptly, and every event is managed with care and attention. Our team is supported by experienced chefs with a deep understanding of local and international cuisines. With over 10 years of experience, our chefs craft menus tailored to your needs, whether you're looking for classic Filipino dishes or innovative culinary creations. We work closely with our clients to ensure their menu is not only delightful but also fits within their budget. To support our growth and continuously improve our service, De Las Armas employs a dedicated team of trained professionals who ensure that all aspects of your event run smoothly. Our staff undergoes regular training in food safety and customer service, ensuring we meet the highest standards for every occasion. With more than 50 team members contributing to our success, we are proud to be a trusted name in catering and event services in the region.
