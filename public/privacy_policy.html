At Delas Armas Catering, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our catering services or visit our website.

Information We Collect

When you book our catering services or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Event details including date, location, guest count, and menu preferences
  • Dietary restrictions and food allergy information
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • IP address, browser type, and pages visited on our website

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology during transmission
  • Personal information is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Only authorized personnel have access to your personal data
  • We conduct regular security assessments and system updates
  • Payment information is processed through PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete information be updated
  • Right to Deletion — request that your personal information be deleted
  • Right to Object — opt out of direct marketing or certain types of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability — receive your data in a structured, transferable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Have Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, feel free to reach out to us directly.
